🌍 Personal Venture
Learn Through Exploring

Learn Through Exploring is a social venture focused on helping youth understand career possibilities beyond traditional post-secondary pathways.

Too many young people feel pressured into post-secondary education without first understanding where their passions lie.

This initiative shares the stories of bold-hearted individuals who explored their options and discovered careers aligned with their passions.

Goals include:

Highlighting alternative career journeys

Encouraging exploration and curiosity

Helping youth make more informed life decisions

Demonstrating that meaningful work comes from pursuing what you care about
